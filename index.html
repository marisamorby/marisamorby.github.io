FIX YOUR OWN BACK PROJECT SUMMARY

Copywriting

I rewrote the site pages for Fix Your Own Back to make the information more understandable to the end user. Before, the wording was very technical and medical, so people weren't sure if the solution was right for them. Afterward, the copy was much easier to understand.

UX

I changed the flow of the site so that users could focus on only one action on each page. Many users were middle-aged and above, so making the site simple to navigate, with only one action option on each page, plus clear navigation, helped the user get to the sign up page easily.

EFFIFIT PROJECT SUMMARY

Copywriting

The main demographic for this business was young women, just out of college, or about three years in to their first job. They were having trouble staying fit and active because of the demands of work and personal life. I wrote the copy to show that staying fit, or gettinb back into shape, didn't have to be hard or take up all your time.

UX

I chose the design for these pages based on the fact that these users didn't have a lot of time to go searching around the site for the answer to their problem. They wanted to know what action they could take now that would help work toward results.
